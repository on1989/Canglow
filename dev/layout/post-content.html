Doors & Windows Installation & Replacement Company

Specialized Provider of Energy Efficient Doors and Windows

Canglow specializes in providing the highest quality 100% lead-free uPVC windows with double/triple glazing and steel/fiberglass doors for clients in Edmonton and the rest of Alberta. With the added strength, you can be assured of their longevity even in the rough Canadian weather. Come rain or frost, your windows and doors will hold good for many years to come.

Precise craftsmanship and attention to detail fused with intricate designs bring forth lush, beautiful windows that add value to your home.

Environment sustainability is something we take very seriously, and we ensure that we do our bit for the environment. 100% of our used materials are recycled, so that we can reduce our waste disposal and make the world a greener place. Behind our conscientious efforts to maintain quality is a green mentality that guides our every endeavour.

Our doors and windows are energy efficient, and each product has passed the energy rating test to help conserve energy and reduce your expenditure. Certified by Energy Star and NAFS, our products help reduce energy loss in winters and gain in warmer months.

As a company we have ensured the highest quality of our products and services, as that is the only driving factor that sets us apart from our competition. Our fenestration products provide a permanent air and water tight seal and all our glass units are double or triple sealed for advanced insulation. This also is a major contributing factor in energy efficiency.

Our goal is to bring you the world class products at the most competitive prices in the local market, and to achieve that goal, we offer windows manufactured by the best production equipment from the industry leader URBAN machinery.
